1. **brown-tee-1.jpg** - Brown classic plain t-shirt

3. **newyork-tee.jpg** - New York graphic t-shirt
4. **humble-tee.jpg** - "Be Humble" graphic t-shirt
5. **rules-tee.jpg** - Urban Kings "RULES" t-shirt

## 📝 Image Requirements

- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Recommended Size**: 800x800px or larger
- **File Names**: Must match exactly as listed above
- **Quality**: High quality images recommended
